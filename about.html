About AAE

Association of Aeronautical Engineers

Our Legacy

The Association of Aeronautical Engineers (AAE) is the official student body of the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University. Established in 1953, we have been at the forefront of aerospace education and innovation for over seven decades.

Our Mission

To foster innovation, leadership, and excellence in aerospace engineering by providing students with opportunities for technical growth, industry exposure, and research collaboration.

Our Vision

To be a premier student association that shapes the future of aerospace in India by nurturing technically competent engineers with a passion for aerospace innovation.

Milestones in the history of AAE

1953

Foundation

AAE was established as the official student body of the Department of Aerospace Engineering at MIT.

1972

First National Symposium

Organized the first national-level technical symposium on aerospace engineering.

1999

Aeromodelling Club

Launched the aeromodelling club which later became one of the most active student clubs.

2010

Industry Partnership Program

Estructured formal partnerships with leading aerospace companies for student internships.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched virtual events and online workshops during the pandemic, expanding our reach nationally.
