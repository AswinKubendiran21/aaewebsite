Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of AAE?

Membership is open to all students of MIT Campus. You can register through our membership portal or visit our office in the Aerospace Engineering department. Annual membership fee is â‚¹500 for students.

Can students from other colleges participate in AAE events?

Yes, most of our events, especially FLIGHT 26, are open to students from all colleges across India. Some workshops may have limited seats, so early registration is recommended.

How can I register for FLIGHT 26?

Registration for FLIGHT 26 can be done through our website. Click on the "Register Now" button on the FLIGHT 26 page and fill out the registration form. Payment can be made online.

Does AAE offer internship opportunities?

Yes, AAE collaborates with various aerospace industries to provide internship opportunities for our members. We also have an alumni network that helps with internship placements.

How can I get updates about upcoming events?

Follow us on social media (links in footer) and check our website regularly. You can also subscribe to our newsletter by contacting us at aero_association@mitindia.edu.
