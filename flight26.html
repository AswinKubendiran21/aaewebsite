About FLIGHT 26

FLIGHT 26 is the flagship national-level technical symposium organized by the Association of Aeronautical Engineers, Department of Aerospace Engineering, MIT Campus, Anna University. This two-day event brings together students, researchers, and industry professionals from across India to explore the latest advancements in aerospace technology.
