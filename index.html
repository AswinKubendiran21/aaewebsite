Who We Are

The Association of Aeronautical Engineers (AAE) is a student-run body that empowers and upskills students in aeronautics. Managed by students, for students, AAE offers hands-on experience in event planning, finance management, workshops, and technical activities. This structure enhances subject knowledge while building leadership, teamwork, and organizational skills, helping students grow into confident professionals ready for the aerospace industry and beyond.
